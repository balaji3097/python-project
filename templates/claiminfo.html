Claim Procedure
  1. 1. The loss or damage should be reported to the insurer immediately.

  2. 2. On receipt of claim intimation, the insurer will forward a claim form.

  3. 3. Submit the completed claim form along with an estimate of the loss to the insurer. It is preferable to submit an itemized estimate with separate values.

  4. 4. The insurer will arrange for an inspection of the damaged items to assess the loss. In case of major losses, a specialist-licensed surveyor is deputed.

  5. 5. The insured has to provide the required documents to substantiate the extent of loss.

  6. 6. In case the cause of loss is not established, it is for the insured to prove that the loss or damage has occurred due to an insured peril.

  7. 7. On agreement of claim amount between the insured and the insurer, the claim is settled.

  8. 8. Excess as stated as per the Policy terms and condition will be deducted from the claim payable.

  9. 9. Notice of an accident (not necessarily a claim) involving third parties should be reported to the insurers.

  10. 10.Every accident involving third parties is required to be reported to the police. M.V.Act provides that a third party victim can proceed against the insurers directly.
